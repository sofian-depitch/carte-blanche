The Method

"The strategy is yours.
How it lands, leave it to me."

70% substance. 30% form. The work happens upstream of the speech: angle, narrative arc, logic of argument, anticipation of objections.

The Protocol

Five phases.
End to end.

Phase 001
Strategic Reframing

Map the audience. Who supports. Who detracts. The angle, sourced and defensible, that repositions the speech before a single slide is touched.

Phase 002
Narrative Architecture

Isolate the single idea the audience must leave with. Strip everything that competes with it. Define the one thread that ties everything together.

Phase 003
Argument Sequencing

Engineer the argument structure. Control when they agree, when they question, and when they concede. Sequence is leverage.

Phase 004
Adversarial Pressure

Attack the argument before the audience does. Surface every weakness, every ambiguity, every line that invites the wrong question.

Phase 005
Full Simulation

Rehearsal under real conditions. Pacing, presence, authority. Every pause, every answer to the question you don't want asked.
