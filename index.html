The Method

“The strategy is yours.
How it lands, leave it to me.”

70% substance. 30% form. The work happens upstream of the pitch: angle, narrative arc, logic of argument, anticipation of objections.

The Protocol

Five phases.
End to end.

Phase N°01
Strategic Reframing.

Map the audience. Who supports. Who detracts. Find the angle that repositions the pitch before a single slide is touched.

Phase N°02
Narrative Architecture.

Build the skeleton. The opening that earns attention. The single idea they must leave with. The close that locks the verdict.

Phase N°03
Argument Sequencing.

Order the proofs. Control when they agree, when they question, when they concede. Sequence is leverage.

Phase N°04
Adversarial Pressure.

Attack the argument before the audience does. Surface every weakness, every ambiguity, every line that invites the wrong question.

Phase N°05
Full Simulation.

Rehearsal under real conditions. Pacing, presence, authority. Every pause, every answer to the question you don't want asked.

The result
You walk in sharp.

Every word precise. Every minute dense. A message built to hit under any pressure.
